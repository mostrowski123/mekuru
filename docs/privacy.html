Privacy Policy

Effective: March 1, 2026

Data Stored on Device

All core reading data - books, dictionaries, saved words, and reading progress - is stored locally on your device. Mekuru does not upload, sync, or transmit that core reading data to any server.

OCR Accounts and Authentication

Mekuru does not require registration to use its core reader, dictionary, and vocabulary features. When you use OCR features, the app uses Firebase Authentication to create an app-specific authenticated identity. This may be an anonymous account created automatically for OCR usage, or a Google-linked account if you choose to link one to restore OCR purchases.

Crash Reporting and Feedback

Mekuru uses Sentry for crash reporting and user-submitted feedback. When a crash occurs or you submit feedback, diagnostic information may be sent to Sentry, including stack traces, device model, and operating system version. This data is used only for debugging and improving the app and is not used for advertising or behavioral tracking.

OCR Data and Billing Requests

When you use OCR, page images and billing-related requests are sent to the currently configured OCR server. If you use the built-in Mekuru OCR server, requests are sent to Mekuru-operated services hosted on the Modal platform to verify OCR access and page credits. Images uploaded to the built-in OCR service are processed in-memory for OCR and are not saved after processing. OCR request data on the built-in service is handled as ephemeral processing data rather than long-term stored content. If you configure a custom OCR server, OCR images and related requests are sent to that server instead.

Third-Party Services

In addition to Sentry, Mekuru uses Firebase Authentication for OCR features and may contact Google Play billing services for OCR purchases on Android. These services are used only to support OCR access, purchase restoration, and billing state.

No Behavioral Analytics

Mekuru does not include advertising SDKs or behavioral analytics. Your reading habits and app usage are not tracked for advertising purposes.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page.
