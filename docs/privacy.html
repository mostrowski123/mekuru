Privacy Policy

Effective: February 19, 2026

Data Stored on Device

All your data — books, dictionaries, saved words, and reading progress — is stored locally on your device. Mekuru does not upload, sync, or transmit your reading data to any server.

No Accounts

Mekuru does not require registration or sign-in. There are no user accounts, and no personal information is collected to use the app.

Crash Reporting & Feedback

Mekuru uses Sentry for crash reporting and user-submitted feedback. When a crash occurs or you submit feedback, diagnostic information may be sent to Sentry, including stack traces, device model, and operating system version. This data is used solely for debugging and improving the app. It is not shared with any other third parties and is not used for advertising or tracking purposes.

No Analytics

Mekuru does not include any behavioral analytics, advertising SDKs, or usage tracking. Your reading habits and app usage are not monitored or recorded.

No Other Third-Party Services

Beyond Sentry for crash reporting, Mekuru does not connect to any third-party services. The app is designed to function entirely offline.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
