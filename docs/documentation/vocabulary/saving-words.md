# Saving & Managing Words

Mekuru lets you build a personal vocabulary list from words you look up while reading.

## Saving a Word

When a dictionary lookup is open, tap the **save** button to add the word to your vocabulary list. Mekuru stores:

- **Expression** - the saved headword
- **Reading** - kana pronunciation
- **Definitions** - the glossary text shown in the lookup
- **Context sentence** - the sentence captured from the reader when available
- **Date saved** - when the word was added

Duplicate detection prevents the same expression and reading pair from being saved twice.

The **Vocabulary** tab is a chronological review list rather than a searchable index.

- Entries are shown newest first.
- Tap an entry to expand it and view full definitions, context, and saved date.
- Swipe left on an entry to delete it.
- Tap the export icon to enter selection mode for CSV export.

## Export Entry Point

When you tap the export icon, Mekuru switches into selection mode so you can choose exactly which entries to export. For the full export flow, see [Exporting to Anki](vocabulary/anki-export.md).
